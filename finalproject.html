SYMPTOMS

COVID-19 affects different people in different ways. Most infected people will develop mild to moderate illness and recover without hospitalization.

Most common symptoms:

  • Fever
  • Cough
  • Tiredness

Less common symptoms:

  • Sore throat
  • Diarrhoea
  • Headache
  • Loss of taste or smell

Serious symptoms:

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Chest pain or pressure
  • Loss of speech or movement

PRECAUTIONS

Take precautions for your loved ones rather than yourself.
For them your life is worth much more than you actually think!!

Wear Mask:

Wear a 3-ply mask to prevent direct contact with virus.

Wash you hands:

Wash/sanitize your hands regularily to get rid of virus if it accidently land onto your hand.

Mantain social distancing:

You must remain 6 feet apart from everyone at all times to prevent the spread of virus.

Get vaccinated:

Vaccine is already tested and proven safe. So, when its your turn don't hesitate to get vaccinated.

Use face shield:

Whenever in contact with a contaminated person, you must use a face shield to prevent direct splash onto your face.

Stay home:

It is obviously the best way to prevent yourself from catching the virus. Don't leave your home unless it is really urgent.
